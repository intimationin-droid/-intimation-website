FAQ

Common questions

What is an ePost intimation and is it legally valid?
+
ePost is India Post's digital messaging service that delivers messages as printed letters to any address in India. It is legally recognized and admissible as proof of communication in courts of law.
How long does processing take?
+
Most orders are processed within 5-15 minutes after payment confirmation. You can track your order status on this website using your vehicle number or loan number.
Which banks are supported?
+
We support 17+ banks including HDFC, CHOLA, Bajaj Finance, HDB, Mahindra Finance, TVS Credit, Tata, SK Finance, Manapuram, IndusInd Bank, IndoStar, IKF, Hinduja, Hero Fincorp, HDB Finance, and AU Small Finance Bank.
What if my order fails?
+
In case of any processing failure, our team will manually review and process your order. You will be notified immediately via the contact number provided in the form.
Can I submit data via WhatsApp message or photo?
+
Yes. You can upload a PDF document, paste raw WhatsApp text, or upload a screenshot of your bank app. Our AI system extracts all required fields automatically.
